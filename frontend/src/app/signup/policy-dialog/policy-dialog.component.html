Users & Roles Policy

Every user is initially registered as a “Professional” to CareerBridge. Later on, users can fill an application to acquire the following roles:

1. Recruiter Recruits professionals to fill job positions

Recruiters can post job positions and view the list of professionals who applied for the job.

2. Career Expert Provides career advice to professionals

Career experts provide career advice to professionals.

3. Skill Assessor Rate the skills of professionals

Skill assessors rate the skills of professionals.
